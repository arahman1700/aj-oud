Primary Logo

The AJ monogram sits within an eight-pointed star frame formed by two overlapping rotated squares, with gold strokes on the Dome Green background.

Clear Space

Maintain a minimum clear space equal to the height of the letter "A" on all sides of the logo.

Color Usage

Do

  • Use Dome Green as the primary brand color
  • Pair Regal Gold with dark backgrounds for contrast
  • Use Marble Cream for page backgrounds
  • Use Night Charcoal for body text
  • Apply Soft Gold for subtle highlights

Don't

  • Place gold text on Marble Cream (low contrast)
  • Use more than 3 brand colors in one composition
  • Apply colors at reduced opacity below 40%
  • Substitute brand colors with similar shades
  • Use gradients for body text or small elements

Warm Lighting

Color temperature 3000K-4000K. Golden-hour warmth. Avoid cool or clinical lighting.

Textures & Materials

Oud wood grain, marble surfaces, woven textiles, brass/gold metalwork. Natural materials only.

Composition

Rule of thirds. Generous negative space. Shallow depth of field. Low angles for grandeur.

Color Grading

Warm shadows, desaturated midtones. Lifted blacks for luxury. Gold highlights where possible.

Smoke & Atmosphere

Incense smoke against dark backgrounds for dramatic, mystical effect.

Forbidden

No harsh flash. No cluttered backgrounds. No over-saturation. No stock-photo aesthetics.

Brand Voice & Tone

How AJ communicates across all digital platforms.

Refined, Not Pretentious

Speak with quiet confidence. Use elegant language without being inaccessible. Every word should feel considered and intentional.

Rooted in Heritage

Reference Madinah's rich tradition naturally. Weave Arabic phrases authentically. Honor the craft's history without being didactic.

Sensory & Evocative

Engage the senses. Describe scents as journeys. Use imagery of smoke, wood, warmth, and sacred spaces.

Bilingual Harmony

Arabic and English should feel equally native. Never treat Arabic as decorative. Both languages carry the brand's full meaning.
